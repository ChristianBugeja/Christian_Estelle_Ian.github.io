A Houseplant Retail Experience

Branding

Potted Goods is a plant destination for the contemporary consumer. Potted Goods is not simply a plant retailer, but rather, an experience provider that seeks to bridge the gap between plants and humans by means of creative innovation. Quality of interaction is at the heart of the brand strategy and interface. Each touchpoint, from the email signature to the packaging solution, stems out of a bold and versatile visual language and is designed with meticulous attention to detail, and with efficacy and aesthetic value in mind.
